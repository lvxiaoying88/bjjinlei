Frequently Asked Questions

What products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of fresh produce including fruits and vegetables, agricultural products, daily necessities, and consumer goods. Our mobile app also supports inventory management for various product categories.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we provide international shipping services. We have established logistics partnerships to ensure timely and safe delivery of products to various countries worldwide.

How can I download your mobile app?

Our mobile management app is available on both Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Simply search for "Bingtangxin" or "Bingtangxin Trading" to find and download our application.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment platforms. Contact us for specific payment options based on your region.

How can I become a partner?

We welcome partnerships with farmers, suppliers, distributors, and businesses. Please contact our partnership team at lina@bjbingtang.com to discuss collaboration opportunities.

Do you offer bulk orders?

Yes, we specialize in both wholesale and retail services. We offer competitive pricing for bulk orders and provide customized solutions for enterprise clients.
